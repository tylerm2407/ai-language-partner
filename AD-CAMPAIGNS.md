**App:** Fluenci — AI-Powered Language Learning

**Recommended Platforms:** Meta (Instagram/Facebook), TikTok, Google Ads, Twitter/X

---

## Campaign Strategy Overview

Fluenci competes in the language learning app space against Duolingo, Babbel, Busuu, and Rosetta Stone. The core differentiator is **real AI conversation practice** — not pre-scripted dialogues, but actual Claude-powered tutoring that adapts to the learner's level, corrects mistakes in real-time, and scores pronunciation. Most competitors offer canned responses; Fluenci offers a real conversation partner available 24/7.

The campaign strategy uses a three-stage funnel: (1) awareness via short-form video showing the AI conversation experience, (2) consideration via testimonials, feature demos, and comparison content, (3) conversion via free trial urgency, limited-time offers, and retargeting. TikTok and Instagram Reels are the primary awareness channels given the app's younger-skewing audience. Google Search captures high-intent "learn Spanish" queries.

Budget should skew heavily toward Meta and TikTok (70% combined) since language learning is an impulse/aspiration purchase driven by visual content, not search intent alone. Google captures the remaining high-intent traffic.

## Testing Plan

### Priority Order
1. **Audience:** Test travel vs career vs casual learner segments (week 1-2)
2. **Creative concept:** AI conversation demo vs streak/gamification vs comparison (week 2-3)
3. **Headline:** "Learn [Language]" vs "Speak [Language]" vs "Practice [Language]" (week 3-4)
4. **CTA:** "Download Free" vs "Start Learning" vs "Try AI Tutor" (week 4)

### Rules
- Minimum 1,000 impressions per variant before judging
- Kill ads at 2x target CPA
- Scale winners by 20% budget increments
- Refresh creative every 2-3 weeks to avoid fatigue
- Test one variable at a time
